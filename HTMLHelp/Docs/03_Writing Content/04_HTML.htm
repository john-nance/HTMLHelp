HTML Content

You can also write content in HTML, and freely mix Markdown and HTML pages.

The conly constraint is that pages have extensions of ".md" for Markdown pages and ".htm" or ".html" for HTML pages.

And yes this page is in HTML, whilst all the other pages are in Markdown.

I find Markdown quicker to write and edit - but it's up to you!

Warning: pureXML does not check or clean content - this is your responsibility as the author. It's your system and you can embed javascripts plus anything else, good or bad that HTML supports.
